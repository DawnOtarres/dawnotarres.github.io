About Me

Aurora, Also known as Dawn Otarres, a young artists who aspires to go to art school and study the animation industry. Aurora since a very young age has always been intriuged by the horror genre and the arts, all starting with a simple doodle in her family's apartment wall. She is currently writing a horror book, and working with her co-creator Bruna, to make an animated horror series.
